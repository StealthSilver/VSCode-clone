Code efficiently
Copilot is your AI pair programmer infused
directly into the editor. Just start typing, and
Copilot will complete your thought by offering a
completion on the current line or (sometimes) a
whole new block of code. Just like a coworker,

none of Copilot's suggestions. Copilot supports
a broad range of languages and frameworks.
